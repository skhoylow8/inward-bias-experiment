Debriefing Sheet:

The purpose of this study is to investigate the a visual phenomenon called inward bias, which is the tendency for people to prefer aesthetic compositions that have figures facing towards the center of a frame.

This study examined whether this phenomenon of leaving more space in the figures' facing direction can be explained by people's expectation to see what will happen in the future. This kind of study can help us understand some of the principles in visual perception.
